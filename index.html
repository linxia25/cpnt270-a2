Structube

Structube is a Canadian family-owned company, and now has more than 50 branches across Canada, with a low price as its highlight!

The reason why Structube is the "H&M" in furniture stores is that many of its styles and models are made by referring to and imitating big-name and luxury furniture.

The style is modern, the quality is good, and the price is similar to IKEA.

  • Established:1974
  • Website: www.structube.com

HomeSense

HomeSense is a brand of TJX company. You can often find some famous brand furniture at HomeSense, but the prices are very cheap.

If Structube is known for "low cheap", then HomeSense is known for "off cheap".

The specialty of this store is that it usually sells some discounted, off-season big-name furniture at low prices. What's more, the goods that arrive in the store every week are different, and you need to visit frequently.

  • Established:2001
  • Website: www.homesense.ca

EQ3

After talking about economical furniture, let's take everyone to the world of higher-grade furniture.

First of all, we recommend EQ3, a local Canadian brand from Winnipeg.

This brand takes the simple and modern style that is popular nowadays. EQ3 supports private customization, and its workmanship is good. Leather art and cloth art are its expertise.

  • Established:2001
  • Website: www.eq3.com

CB2

CB2 is the most cost-effective brand of furniture. CB2's position in the furniture industry is equivalent to Aritzia's position in the fashion industry.

Its furniture has a strong American style, and everything is designed with care and small ingenuity in it. The sense of design is very strong, and you can't help but look at it.

  • Established:1962
  • Website: www.cb2.com
